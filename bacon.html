    Bacon

    Bacon is a breakfast side dish staple. Your breakfast isn't complete without some bacon.

    Materials:

    • Small frying pan
    • Spatula

    Ingredients:

    • Bacon
    1. Gather materials. For help finding materials and ingredients, consult the guide to my kitchen
    2. Place frying pan on the stove and place enough bacon in the pan to cover the bottom, leaving a small amount of space between each piece of bacon.
    3. Turn heat on medium-high and cook bacon, turning once, for seven to ten minutes, or until crisp and brown.
