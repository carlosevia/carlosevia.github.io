    Finding First Gear

    Finding, and then shifting to, first gear involves some careful work with the pedals. You need to pay attention to the clutch, break, and accelerator.

    You will want to have your left foot on the clutch and your right foot on the break before starting the car.
    1. Start the car and let off the emergency brake.
    2. Shift the car to neutral.
    3. Shift into first gear, continuing to press both the clutch and brake firmly.
      Got all that? Great, put it all together and...
    4. Take your foot off of the brake and accelerate slowly while simultaneously easing off the clutch.
