    Feeding a monkey

    Quick introduction to the art and science of monkey feeding

    Feed monkeys only when they are hungry

    1. Let the monkey know that food is coming
      1. Address the monkey in perfect English. Monkeys do not speak Spanish
      2. Announce food only if it's coming soon
    2. Serve food cold. Monkeys do not like hot food
      Note: Monkeys are fun, but they can be mean! Be careful when handling monkeys.
